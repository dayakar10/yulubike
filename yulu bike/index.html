  • How to Yulu

  • Download YULU app

    Download the Yulu app from the
    Appstore or the Playstore

  • Locate a Yulu

    Use the Yulu app to find the closest
    vehicle to you or look out for a Yulu Zone
    around you.

  • Scan the QR code

    To unlock the vehicle, simply scan the QR code
    located on the panel.

  • Ride Safely

    Enjoy the Yulu ride but remember to
    abide by all the traffic laws and parking
    policies.

  • End your ride

    To end your ride, park the vehicle at
    a Yulu Zone, lock it and click on the End
    button on your app.
